You can choose from 50 different crosshair overlays. The software lets you change the color, size, and shape of these overlays. These overlays stay on your screen while you play. They do not interact with game files, so they remain safe to use while streaming.

### System Optimization
The FPS booster scans your system settings to find performance gains. It adjusts background processes to clear resources for the game. This helps your computer maintain a steady frame rate during heavy action.

### Sound Settings
The audio enhancer changes how your computer processes game sounds. It compresses loud noises and boosts quiet sounds like footsteps. You can adjust the intensity of these effects to match your headset.

### Configuration Management
You can save your settings into a file. This tool allows you to import and export these files. If you change your computer or want to share settings with a friend, you can load your profiles quickly.
